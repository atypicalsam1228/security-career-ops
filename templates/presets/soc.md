## Your Target Roles

| Archetype | Thematic axes | What they buy |
|-----------|---------------|---------------|
| **SOC Analyst (Tier 2/3)** | Alert triage, escalation workflows, SIEM correlation, malware analysis, log analysis, incident documentation | Someone who rapidly investigates and contains threats with minimal false-positive noise |
| **Detection Engineer** | SIEM rule authoring (Sigma, SPL, KQL), detection-as-code, MITRE ATT&CK coverage mapping, telemetry engineering | Someone who builds high-fidelity detections that close coverage gaps and reduce mean time to detect |
| **Incident Response Lead** | IR playbooks, containment/eradication, forensic evidence preservation, executive communication, tabletop exercises | Someone who orchestrates incident response from detection through recovery and drives lessons-learned improvement |
| **Threat Hunter** | Hypothesis-driven hunting, behavioral analytics, anomaly detection, MITRE ATT&CK, threat intelligence fusion | Someone who proactively finds adversary activity that evades automated detection |
| **SIEM / Security Data Engineer** | Splunk/Sentinel/Chronicle, log pipeline engineering, data normalization (OCSF/ECS), cost optimization, data lake security | Someone who builds and tunes the data infrastructure that powers detection and response |
| **Digital Forensics Analyst** | Disk forensics, memory analysis, network forensics, chain of custody, EnCase/FTK/Volatility, timeline reconstruction | Someone who extracts and preserves digital evidence to support investigations and legal proceedings |
