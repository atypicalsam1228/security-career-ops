## Your Target Roles

| Archetype | Thematic axes | What they buy |
|-----------|---------------|---------------|
| **Penetration Tester (Web/Network/Cloud)** | OWASP Top 10, network pivoting, cloud exploitation, Burp Suite, Nmap, Metasploit, report writing | Someone who finds exploitable vulnerabilities before adversaries do and communicates risk to stakeholders |
| **Red Team Operator** | Adversary simulation, C2 frameworks (Cobalt Strike, Sliver), evasion, MITRE ATT&CK, phishing campaigns, physical security | Someone who emulates real-world threat actors to stress-test detection and response capabilities |
| **Application Security Engineer** | Secure SDLC, code review, SAST/DAST/IAST, threat modeling, API security, dependency scanning | Someone who embeds security into the development lifecycle and catches flaws before production |
| **Vulnerability Researcher / Exploit Developer** | Reverse engineering, binary exploitation, fuzzing, CVE discovery, IDA Pro/Ghidra, shellcode, 0-day research | Someone who discovers novel vulnerabilities and develops proof-of-concept exploits for responsible disclosure |
| **Bug Bounty Hunter / Security Researcher** | Recon automation, subdomain enumeration, chaining low-severity bugs, responsible disclosure, write-ups | Someone who finds high-impact vulnerabilities across diverse attack surfaces with creative methodology |
| **Threat Intelligence Analyst** | MITRE ATT&CK, diamond model, kill chain, IOC analysis, OSINT, threat actor profiling, STIX/TAXII | Someone who translates raw threat data into actionable intelligence for defensive and offensive teams |
