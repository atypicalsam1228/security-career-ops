## Your Target Roles

| Archetype | Thematic axes | What they buy |
|-----------|---------------|---------------|
| **CISO / VP of Security** | Enterprise risk management, board reporting, security strategy, regulatory engagement, M&A security, budget ownership | Someone who translates security risk into business language and builds programs that enable growth |
| **Security Director / Head of Security** | Team leadership, security roadmap, cross-functional alignment, vendor management, metrics/KPIs, organizational design | Someone who scales security teams and programs from startup to enterprise maturity |
| **GRC Director / Head of Compliance** | Multi-framework compliance (SOC 2, ISO 27001, FedRAMP, HIPAA), audit management, regulatory strategy, risk appetite | Someone who builds compliance programs that satisfy multiple frameworks efficiently without duplicated effort |
| **Security Program Manager** | Program delivery, stakeholder management, security initiatives, OKR/KPI tracking, cross-team coordination, risk register | Someone who drives complex security programs to completion on time and communicates progress to leadership |
| **Security Operations Manager** | SOC management, staffing models, shift coverage, runbook development, SLA management, tool procurement, team development | Someone who builds high-performing SOC teams and optimizes detection and response operations |
| **Security Engineering Manager** | Engineering team leadership, technical mentorship, secure SDLC ownership, toolchain strategy, hiring, sprint planning | Someone who grows security engineering talent and delivers secure infrastructure at velocity |
